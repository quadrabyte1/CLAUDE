#### Summary

A simple desk sign with cut out and tilted text.

You may need to adjust the text vertical offset to get a pleasing result, because mathematical centering usually looks wrong.

Opening in the OpenSCAD customizer on your machine will be much faster than using the Thingiverse customizer online..

And you may get warnings about the STL being non-manifold (because of internal voids and touching polygon edges) - but so far it prints cleanly in every program I've tried.

YFMV: Your Fonts May Vary.

#### Print Settings

- Printer brand:
  Bambu Lab
- Printer:
  Bambu P1S
- Supports:
  Yes
- Notes:
  If you can use variable layer heights in your slicer: use large layers until you get to the start of the visible (tilted) characters, then switch to smaller layers. This greatly speeds up and improves the print.
