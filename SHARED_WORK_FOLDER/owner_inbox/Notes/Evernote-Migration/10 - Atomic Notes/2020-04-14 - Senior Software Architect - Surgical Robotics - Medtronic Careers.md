# Senior Software Architect - Surgical Robotics

#### [Boston, Massachusetts, United States](https://jobs.medtronic.com/jobs/76415/other-jobs-matching/location-only)

[](https://jobs.medtronic.com/landingpages/engineering-opportunities-at-medtronic-19) Category  
[Engineering](https://jobs.medtronic.com/landingpages/engineering-opportunities-at-medtronic-19)

200004PB Requisition \#

6 days ago Post Date

**Careers that Change Lives**

You will join a team of mechanical, electrical and software engineers responsible for developing Medtronic’s Robotic Assisted Surgery platform through commercial launch and production scale-up.

**Impact patient outcomes.  Come for a job, stay for a career**

The Minimally Invasive Therapies Group strives to enable earlier diagnosis, better treatment, faster complication-free recovery, and enhanced patient outcomes through less invasive surgical solutions.

SURGICAL INNOVATIONS set the standard for Minimally Invasive Surgery (MIS) by creating innovative surgical products and services that focus on obesity and diseases and conditions of the gastrointestinal tract, lung, abdominal wall, pelvic region, and the head and neck.

**A Day in the Life**** **

This position is in the Boston, MA Seaport District, as Sr Software Engineer for Software Architecture you will collaborate with the Lead Architect to maintain the Software Architecture Model (UML, SysML) and documentation (JAMA) and work with developers to align their implementation with the architecture.

****You will ****

- Support maintaining the Software Architecture Model (UML, SysML) using Enterprise Architect

- Manage Software Items in JAMA

- Collaborate with developers to align Architecture and Detailed Design through Design and Implementation reviews

- Improve the Software Architecture by understanding and applying our domain of Surgical Robotic Software (from User Needs, to Requirements and down to implementation challenges)

**Must haves: Minimum requirements**:

- Minimum of 4 years of relevant experience, or advanced degree with a minimum of 2 years relevant experience

**Nice to haves:**

- A Bachelor’s Degree in a technical discipline and 5+ years of relevant experience.

- Solid design and architecture skills with UML

- Experience developing software for multi-threaded, multi-processor software systems. 

- Have a good understanding of software development methodologies

- Strong C++ skills.

- Strong UML / SysML skills

- Experience developing software for embedded, real-time systems, ideally running QNX

- Experience with Middleware DDS, Unix OS (Ubuntu), and network protocols (TCP/IP)

- Experience with software infrastructure development to support data logging, network communication, process management, safety implementation, software installation, network configuration as well as working with build and continuous integration teams.

- Experience programming multiprocessor medical devices.

- Good understanding of 62304 and regulated will defined SDLC.

**Working Conditions:** 

Standard Office setting in South Boston.  Some limited travel may be required.

**About Medtronic**

Together, we can change healthcare worldwide. At Medtronic, we push the limits of what technology, therapies and services can do to help alleviate pain, restore health and extend life.  We challenge ourselves and each other to make tomorrow better than yesterday. It is what makes this an exciting and rewarding place to be.

We want to accelerate and advance our ability to create meaningful innovations - but we will only succeed with the right people on our team. Let’s work together to address universal healthcare needs and improve patients’ lives. Help us shape the future.
