***To make a reservation through Enterprise Rent-A-Car:***

**Enterprise Rent-A-Car Pin \#: CHR**

• Visit [www.enterprise.com](http://www.enterprise.com/)

• On the [*enterprise.com*](http://enterprise.com/) home page enter your pickup location, date, and time

• Enter your FIAT 500e Pass account number (noted above) in the “Coupon, Customer, or Corporate Number”

   field and click the “search” button

• On the next screen, enter your Enterprise Rent-A-Car Pin \# noted above

• Continue in the reservation path, confirming your pickup/drop-off location and vehicle selection

• Enter your renter information in the “Renter’s Detail” page and click the “Continue” button

• When verifying your information, please enter the Billing Number (noted above) in the “Paying with a Billing Number” box and complete your reservation by clicking the “Book Now” button

 

***To make a reservation through Alamo Rent A Car:***

• Visit [www.alamo.com](http://www.alamo.com/)

• On the [*Alamo.com*](http://Alamo.com) home page select “reservations” in the top navigation menu and then click

   the “Go” button to book a vehicle

• Enter the pickup location, date and time in the “Where?” and “When?” fields

• Under “Discounts” enter your FIAT 500e Pass Account \# (noted above) in the Contract ID field

  and click the “Continue” button

• Select a vehicle for your reservation

• If you choose, please select any optional extras and click the “Continue” button

• Complete the fields in “Your Information”, enter the Billing \# (noted above) in the “Payment Information”

   field, and click “Book It” to complete your reservation

*** ***

***To make a reservation through National Car Rental:***

• Visit [www.nationalcar.com](http://www.nationalcar.com/)

• On the [*nationalcar.com*](http://nationalcar.com/) home page click “Reserve” in the top navigation menu

• Enter the pickup/drop-off location, date, and time under the “Tell us about your trip” section

• Under “Optional rate Information” enter your FIAT 500e Pass Account \# in the Contract ID field

   and check the box to make a reservation using a Central Billing \#

• Once you check the box to make a reservation using a Central Billing \#, please enter the Billing \#

  (noted above) in the “Central Bill/Business Account \# field and click “Continue” to proceed

• Select a vehicle for your reservation

• At the bottom of “Step 3: Select rental options and calculate”, please enter your information

• Click “Continue” to complete your reservation

 

To pick up your vehicle, please bring your reservation confirmation number and a valid driver’s license.

 

You will not receive a physical FIAT 500e Pass card. To avoid an unauthorized rental on your account, we suggest

recording your account number in a safe place and only sharing it with those in charge of making reservations for you.

 

We hope you find this information helpful, and we look forward to providing you with the FIAT 500e Pass.

If you have any questions regarding your FIAT 500e Pass, please contact FIAT USA at 855-411-500e.
