## Notes

- **The 17e replaced the SE line.** Apple introduced the iPhone 17e in March 2026 as the new affordable anchor. There is no current iPhone SE sold new. The 17e doubled entry storage to 256 GB at the same $599 price the SE line held.
- **Only the 17 Pro and Pro Max have the periscope / tetraprism telephoto.** This is the big optical-zoom lens (up to 8x optical-quality). No other model in the lineup can match it — the Air and 17e max out at a 2x optical-quality telephoto, and the regular 17 has no dedicated telephoto at all.
- **Only the 17 Pro Max offers 2 TB storage.** Every other model caps lower.
- **ProMotion (120 Hz) is a Pro/Air/regular-17 feature.** The 16, 16 Plus, and 17e are all locked to 60 Hz displays. For a buyer who wants smooth scrolling, the cheapest ProMotion phone is the $799 iPhone 17.
- **The iPhone 16 line was trimmed, not killed.** Apple kept the iPhone 16 ($699, 128 GB only) and iPhone 16 Plus ($799, 128/256 GB) on sale at reduced prices after the 17 launch, but pulled the higher-storage tiers to avoid overlap with the 17. The iPhone 16 Pro and 16 Pro Max are no longer sold new — they moved to the refurbished store in February 2026.
- **The iPhone Air is the odd one out.** Thinnest iPhone ever, A19 Pro chip, ProMotion, but only a single rear camera (48 MP main with 2x optical-quality crop). It's a design/premium-feel buy, not a camera buy.
- **All current models use USB-C.** Lightning is extinct in the new lineup.
- **All current models run Apple Intelligence.** Even the 17e ships with the full A19 Neural Engine and 8 GB RAM, so no model is locked out of Apple's AI features.

### Quick buyer's-eye-view

- **Cheapest new iPhone:** iPhone 17e — $599
- **Cheapest ProMotion iPhone:** iPhone 17 — $799
- **Best camera system:** iPhone 17 Pro / 17 Pro Max — only models with periscope telephoto
- **Biggest screen:** iPhone 17 Pro Max — 6.9"
- **Thinnest / lightest:** iPhone Air
- **Most affordable Plus-size screen:** iPhone 16 Plus — $799, 6.7"
