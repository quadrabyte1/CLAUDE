## Customizing greetings and settings

If you have call screening enabled, you can record the name people hear when calling you. Click the **gear icon** at the top right of the page, select **Voice settings**, and select the **Voicemail & SMS** tab. Then, click **Record New** to record your name. Specify which phone you want to forward your calls to, and click **Connect**. This will call your selected phone and prompt you to record the name you want callers to hear.

You can also set up a custom voicemail greeting for when contacts who call you are directed to your Google voicemail. To do this, follow the steps below:

1.  

    ☐

    Click **gear icon** at the top right of any page, click **Voice settings**, and select the **Voicemail & SMS** tab.

2.  

    ☐

    In the **Voicemail Greeting** section, click the **Add** link.

3.  

    ☐

    Enter the name for the greeting you're going to record and click **OK**.

4.  

    ☐

    A **Record Greeting** box appears. Select the phone you want to call to record the greeting, from the **Phone to ring** drop-down menu.

5.  

    ☐

    Click **Connect.**

6.  

    ☐

    Pick up the call and record your voicemail greeting when prompted to do so.

7.  

    ☐

    Once you've recorded your voicemail greeting, make sure the voicemail greeting you want to play is selected from the **Voicemail Greeting** drop-down of the **Voicemail & SMS** tab of your Settings.

8.  

    ☐

    Click **Save Changes** at the bottom of the page.

The greeting you've set on the **Voicemail & SMS** tab of your Settings is the general voicemail greeting all callers hear.
