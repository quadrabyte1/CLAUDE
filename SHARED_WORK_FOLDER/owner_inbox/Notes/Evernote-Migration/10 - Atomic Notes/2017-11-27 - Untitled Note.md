Failure Safety System -- The Interviews

\

- ☐

  Joe Cesna and I are working closely together on this project

  - ☐

    Joe has a good deal of experience dealing with fault handling systems in space craft.

- ☐

  A series of 30 minute interviews were undertaken with each of the main "leads" of our various teams.

  \

- ☐

  Initially the intention was to gather failure items from each person but that proved to be impossible in such a short meeting.

  \

- ☐

    There is a new spreadsheet to capture every conceivable failure.

- ☐

  We will follow up with an email by December 1 with some brief instructions and a link to the spreadsheet.
