I am writing to recommend Nathan Gong for admission to the graduate program in Computer Science at Columbia University, Fu Foundation School of Engineering. I have known Nathan for about a year during his employment at Medtronic, Inc. He and I worked together on a project to apply a new data handling system to our robotic surgery system.

Nathan proved to be a quick study. When we started on this project he had no previous experience the new Troika data handling system that was under development in my group. I supervised Nathan's work, attacking a problem that needed solving by our GUI group and to which we wanted to apply the Troika technology. He needed very little in the way of instruction;I just explained the goal and pointed him to the Troika tools he would use. He was quite willing to ask questions and for clarification a few times. But overall, he went off to work by himself to produce a first draft of a solution. We discussed his work, made a few easy improvements, then he took his work to the GUI team. That team was pleased with what he had produced (and surprised at how quickly he had accomplished it). Nathan worked with the GUI group very comfortably then, bringing  his solution into alignment with exactly what they wanted. 

Nathan is bright and eager to learn new things. He was a pleasure to work with and I believe he would be an excellent student in your program. I give him my highest recommendation.
