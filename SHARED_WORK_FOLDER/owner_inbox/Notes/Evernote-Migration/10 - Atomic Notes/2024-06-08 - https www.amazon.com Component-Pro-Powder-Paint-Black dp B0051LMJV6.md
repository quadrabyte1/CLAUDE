### Description

Thinking about making your jigs and lures colorful? Try on this Pro-Tec Powder Paint and have the most attractive fish baits that will definitely get those fish biting onto them. This powder coating paint has virtually no odor and no solvents and it leaves a high gloss finishing on your tackle box fishing accessories in just a few seconds. It allows you to save time as it doesn't require mixing and drying time and it's very easy to clean. You'll also save extra bucks for you don't need a gloss coat or primer coat. Each 2-ounce jar of this premium-quality and high-gloss Pro-Tec Powder Paint can cover 3,000 of 3/8oz jigs. It helps increase the glow properties of any fishing jigs or fishing lures within less than 20 minutes of painting. DIRECTIONS: Shake briefly to fluff paint. Preheat metal lures using a small torch, oven, or any flame for 4-10 seconds depending on size. Quickly swish into powder and remove, knocking off excess. The lure should be hot enough to melt powder within 2-4 seconds after removal. Let cool. For maximum durability, hang and bake in a preheated 350°F (176°C) oven for 20 minutes. Always pop out the eye before curing. IMPORTANT REMINDERS: 1.) After swishing several jigs, the powder will settle. Shake or stir to keep loose and fluffy. Call for production methods using powder. 2.) Store in a cool area. INCLUDES: 1 x 2oz Pro-Tec Powder Paint for Lures and Jigs
