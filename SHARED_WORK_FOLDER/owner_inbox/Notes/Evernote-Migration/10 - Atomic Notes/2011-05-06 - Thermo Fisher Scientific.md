**SC20112104-10065 -- Senior Software Architect Chief Software Engineer, Microsoft Technologies**

Designing and developing the applications for scientists, lab directors, and chemists that are engaged in extending the health profile for all of us is a dream opportunity.  We are looking for those who are passionate to join our senior applications team and produce the high performing, user engrossing applications that will accelerate research and production level interaction with our medical instruments.  This is typically done through the PC that is shipped with the instruments and on many occasions from the scientist’s desktop workstation.  The mainstream use of capacitive touch, cloud comp! uting, and smartphones for monitoring network elements is providing us the opportunity to be the market leader in ease-of-use instruments. We are looking for the innovative, driven people to help us help others who heal the world.

 

***The Senior Software Architect – Chief Software Engineer, Microsoft Technologies*** evolves the existing point applications and infrastructure into a platform or solutions based architecture that solves the analytical needs of the mass spectrometry users. Skilled in the engineering of software to address market scalability requirements, the chief software engineer understands what it takes to move applications (emerging as well as legacy) toward a layered architecture that supports! the scientific community. Working with the applications roadmap as a directional guide you will transition the insights from software engineers and managers into change iterations resulting in software releases that become easy to build on to.  You will be aided by diversified development teams and researchers/collaborators from the top universities and research labs from around the world.  As the leading provider of Mass Spectrometers our software fuels discovery and you will play a significant role in it. 

 

 

This position will provide an opportunity to utilize and enhance your skills with the following technologies:

  Desktop, distributed, and cloud architectures

- ☐

  C# with .NET 4.0

- ☐

  Windows Presentation Foundation (WPF)

- ☐

  Windows Workflow Foundation (WF)

- ☐

  Windows Communication Foundation (WCF)

- ☐

  Visual Studios 2010 and Team Foundation Server 2010

 

**Responsibilities:**

- ☐

  Ability to conceptualize long-term solutions to entrenched systemic problems.

  Synthesize current and emerging market requirements in terms of solutions that can be provided through software acquiring and analyzing data from mass spectrometers.

  Ability to work with and influence senior people in a variety of roles including scientists, engineers, business decision makers, application chemists, and software architects.

  Work with other software team members, the software test group and applications personnel to deliver complete and high quality software products to customers, especially Windows-based applications, according to schedules and specifications

  Work in cross functional teams to help set the direction of the next generation application software

  Mentor and provide direct support to junior engineers

**Minimum Qualifications/Skills:**

  Outstanding Mass Spec scientists (PhD) with demonstrated software development skills will be considered in lieu of five years commercial software development experience.
